About

I am a first-year PhD student at École Normale Supérieure–PSL in Paris, supervised by Giulio Biroli (ENS) and Marc Mézard (Bocconi University). I am based at the Centre de Sciences des Données and at the Laboratoire de Physique de l’ENS (LPENS). I work on diffusion models, a state-of-the-art method in generative AI. My goal is to understand theoretically how they manage to generalize without memorizing the training samples.

Research

Current topic: A statistical physics approach to Diffusion models.

  • Theoretical foundations of Diffusion Models
  • The generalization/memorization transition in diffusion Models
